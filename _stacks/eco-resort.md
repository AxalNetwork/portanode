Eco-Resort is the off-grid hospitality stack we co-designed with a Patagonian lodge group. Guests get hot water, lights, fresh greens, and Wi-Fi without ever seeing a generator.

## Why this stack

- 3× Volt covers a 30-key resort comfortably with seasonal margin.
- Grow keeps the kitchen garden producing year-round at any latitude.
- Cycle turns guest waste into biogas for the kitchen — a real story for the brand book.
