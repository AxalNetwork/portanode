The Off-Grid Clinic is what most ministries-of-health pilot first. It serves a community of ~5,000 with primary care, vaccinations, and 24/7 telemedicine.

## Why this stack

- Care + Flow loop keeps sterile water, hand-wash, and lab supply continuous.
- Cold-chain pharmacy survives 72 h of grid loss on Volt batteries alone.
- Optional Cycle add-on makes the clinic fully closed-loop on greywater and biowaste.
