The Vertical Farm stack is sized for a regional aggregator selling into supermarkets and food-service clients. Four Grow modules, a closed water loop, and a Cycle that turns scrap back into nutrient.

## Why this stack

- Predictable yield: ~8 t / yr of leafy greens regardless of climate.
- 95% water reduction vs. soil-based equivalents thanks to Flow + Cycle loop.
- HACCP-ready packing zone in the Shell, with axalOS lot-tracking from seed to crate.
