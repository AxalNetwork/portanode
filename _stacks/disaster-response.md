The Disaster Response stack ships from one of three pre-staged inventories (Rotterdam, Singapore, Houston). The same kit goes to flood, earthquake, refugee, and conflict scenarios.

## Why this stack

- Air-freightable: any module fits a 747F or An-124 cargo bay.
- Pre-flight tested and sealed — no commissioning lab required on landing.
- Connectivity-first: Core's Starlink uplink works the moment power is up.
