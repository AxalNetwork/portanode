Edge Compute is what you ship when you need a 120 kW IT load 200 km from any data centre. Operators love that the same SKU drops next to a 5G tower, a hydro dam, or a wind farm.

## Why this stack

- Datacenter-layout Shell with hot-aisle containment and N+1 in-rack cooling.
- Dual-uplink Core (Starlink + carrier 5G + fibre) means service-level guarantees even at the edge.
- Volt + grid-tie keeps PUE low and lets you sell capacity back when rates spike.
