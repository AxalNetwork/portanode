The Research Lab stack is the most popular AXAL configuration with universities and government science programs. It's been deployed on three continents, twice north of 70° latitude.

## Why this stack

- **Lab-grade water** out of Flow's RO + UV polishing loop, fed directly to a glassware washer in Shell.
- **HEPA + negative-pressure** option on the lab Shell makes it BSL-2 ready without site retrofit.
- **Two Volts** keeps the freezers and centrifuges alive through any single failure or grid interruption.
