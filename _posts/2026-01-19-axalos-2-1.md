axalOS 2.1 rolled out across the fleet over the last fortnight. Two features are worth calling out.

## Predictive maintenance for Volt and Flow

We've been collecting telemetry from every deployed Volt and Flow for two years. 2.1 is the first release that surfaces **module-level failure forecasts** in the operator console — typically 7–14 days of warning before a coupling, pump, or cell needs attention.

The model is conservative on purpose: false positives waste a service call, false negatives waste a stack day. We tuned for the second.

## Offline LMS deltas for Learn

Learn modules in low-bandwidth deployments now sync **only the delta** for course content updates. A typical refresh dropped from ~2.4 GB to ~84 MB. That makes the difference between "Wi-Fi for an evening" and "Wi-Fi for a week" at sites running on a single Starlink link.

## Rollout

axalOS 2.1 is the new default for new stacks and rolls out automatically over the next 30 days for existing deployments. Roll-back is one click in the console if anything misbehaves.
