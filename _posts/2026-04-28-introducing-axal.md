Today we're opening the AXAL catalog to anyone who can use it. Eight modules, seven reference stacks, and a configurator that lets you build, validate, and quote a complete deployment in under ten minutes.

## What's shipping today

- The eight-module catalog: **Core, Volt, Flow, Grow, Shell, Cycle, Care, Learn.**
- Seven pre-validated stacks across research, healthcare, agriculture, edge compute, hospitality, satellite, and disaster response.
- A configurator with live power, water, and footprint validation.
- Stripe-backed quote-to-deposit checkout, plus operating-lease and IaaS options.

## What's next

The configurator's interactive 3D site planner ships next month. After that, we'll open up the customer portal for fleet management and remote diagnostics. Subscribe to this blog if that's the kind of thing you want to read about.
