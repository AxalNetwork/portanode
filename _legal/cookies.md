This Cookie Notice describes the cookies and similar technologies used on axal.dev.

## 1. Strictly necessary

We use a small number of first-party cookies that are required for the site to function:

- **Session and CSRF tokens** during checkout and configurator use.
- **Cookie-consent state** (`axal.cookie.consent`), to remember your preference.

These cannot be turned off; without them the site does not work.

## 2. Analytics (consent-gated)

If you accept analytics cookies, we use **first-party Plausible-style analytics** (no cross-site tracking, no advertising IDs) to understand how the site is used. This is loaded only after you click "Accept" in the cookie banner.

## 3. What we do not use

- We do **not** use third-party advertising trackers.
- We do **not** use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar profiling cookies.
- We do **not** sell or share cookie data with third parties.

## 4. How to manage

You can change or revoke your consent at any time by clearing the `axal.cookie.consent` value in your browser, or via the banner that re-appears when consent is reset. You can also disable cookies entirely in your browser settings.
