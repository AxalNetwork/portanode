This Refund Policy applies to AXAL hardware orders and to AXAL service contracts. It supplements the [Terms of Sale](/legal/terms-of-sale/).

## 1. Deposits

The 30% order deposit is **fully refundable** if you cancel before AXAL has begun manufacturing (typically 14 days after order acceptance). After manufacturing has begun, the deposit covers AXAL's costs and is non-refundable.

## 2. Factory acceptance test (FAT)

If a module fails its FAT, AXAL will repair or replace it at our expense. If the failure cannot be remedied within 60 days, you may cancel the affected line items and receive a full refund of payments made for those items.

## 3. Shipment

Once a module has shipped, refunds are governed by the warranty terms in the [Terms of Sale](/legal/terms-of-sale/). Returns of shipped modules are accepted only by prior agreement with AXAL.

## 4. Operating leases and IaaS

Operating-lease and IaaS contracts have their own termination and refund clauses. Refer to your signed agreement.

## 5. Software (axalOS) subscriptions

axalOS support subscriptions are billed monthly or annually. Annual prepayments are refundable on a pro-rata basis if you terminate, less any usage and discount-clawback as set out in the order form.

## 6. Disputes

If you believe a refund is due and we disagree, escalation runs through your account engineer, then to <span class="font-mono">commercial@axal.dev</span>, and finally — if unresolved — to the dispute mechanism described in the Terms of Sale.
