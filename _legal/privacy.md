This Privacy Notice describes how AXAL B.V. ("AXAL") collects and uses personal data. AXAL is the controller for personal data processed through axal.dev and our customer-facing systems.

## 1. What we collect

- **Account data:** name, work email, organisation, role, country.
- **Communication data:** support tickets, sales conversations, marketing-email engagement.
- **Telemetry from deployed stacks:** axalOS sends operating telemetry (power, water, fault states) so we can support you. This is associated with the stack, not with named individuals.
- **Web analytics:** if you accept cookies, we use Plausible-style first-party analytics. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

## 2. How we use it

- To deliver, support, and improve the Products.
- To respond to spec-book requests, sales enquiries, and support tickets.
- To send service notices about your deployed stacks.
- To send occasional product updates by email (you can unsubscribe at any time).

## 3. Lawful basis (GDPR)

We rely on contract necessity, legitimate interest, and — where required — consent. Customers and prospects in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland have the rights described in GDPR Articles 15–22.

## 4. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share with:

- **Sub-processors** (cloud hosting, email, helpdesk) under DPA.
- **Authorities** when legally required.
- **Buyers** of all or part of AXAL in the event of a merger or sale; you'll be notified.

## 5. Retention

Account data is retained while the account is active and for 7 years after closure for tax, warranty, and audit purposes. Telemetry is retained for the operational life of the stack plus 12 months.

## 6. Security

AXAL is ISO 27001 certified. axalOS uses signed images, transport-layer encryption, and per-deployment key material.

## 7. International transfers

AXAL stores customer-facing data in the EU and Singapore. Transfers outside the EEA are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent.

## 8. Your rights

To exercise rights of access, correction, deletion, portability, or to lodge a complaint, email <span class="font-mono">privacy@axal.dev</span>. EU residents may also contact their national supervisory authority.
