How it works

From spec to site in four steps.

  1. 01 / Configure

    Pick modules

    Start from a stack template or build from scratch. The configurator validates power, water, and footprint live.

  2. 02 / Quote

    Get a fixed price

    Hardware, shipping tier, install support, and warranty in a single quote. No surprises at the dock.

  3. 03 / Build

    Modules are factory-built, FAT-tested, sealed, and loaded onto a flat-rack or 40-HC for any standard route.

    Two technicians can level, couple, and energise a four-module stack in under a day.
