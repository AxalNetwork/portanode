## The shape of the problem

Edge compute customers usually have the silicon figured out. The hard part is the building: power, cooling, security, and the SLA to back it. AXAL is the building.

## Why this stack works

- **Datacenter-layout Shell** with hot-aisle containment and N+1 in-rack cooling, certified to typical Tier-2 standards.
- **Volt + grid-tie** with grid-forming firmware — graceful failover, no UPS-blink.
- **Dual-uplink Core** (Starlink + carrier 5G + fibre) means service-level guarantees that mean something at the edge.

## Density & power

A standard Edge Compute stack supports **120 kW IT load**. Customers running denser silicon often add a second Volt and a Flow for evaporative supplemental cooling.
