## The mine site problem

Mine sites are remote, regulated, and time-sensitive. Building a camp from scratch typically takes 12–18 months and a small army of contractors, half of whom never show up to a Tier-3 location twice.

## How AXAL changes the math

- **Camp accommodation:** Shell modules in 8-bunk layout, deployable in 48 hours per pod.
- **Occupational health:** Care + Flow gives you a 24/7 health post on-site, with telemedicine to corporate medical.
- **Water treatment:** Flow handles borehole, surface, or shower greywater, with reclamation for dust suppression.
- **Edge compute:** Core + Volt drops a small data centre next to your mill control room.

## Why operators come back

Modules redeploy. When the orebody is exhausted, you crane the stack onto a flat-rack and ship it to the next site. That's a balance-sheet asset, not a remediation cost.
