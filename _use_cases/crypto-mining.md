## Stranded energy is the opportunity

The most economical mining sites in 2026 are next to power that can't reach a load: flared associated gas, curtailed renewables, off-grid hydro. AXAL is engineered exactly for those sites.

## What we ship

- **Edge Compute stack** with an immersion-cooled Shell variant for high-density ASIC fleets.
- **Volt** as the buffer / grid-forming layer that smooths the variability of curtailed wind or solar.
- **Core** runs the pool integration, on-site monitoring, and remote-shutoff for compliance.

## Compliance & PR

We work with operators that publish a credible energy story (curtailment-additionality, methane-mitigation). Customers using AXAL for mining at high-grid-mix locations should expect a tougher conversation; see [Sustainability](/sustainability/).
