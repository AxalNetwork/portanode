## The economics

Most agri co-ops price like commodity producers. AXAL gives a co-op a way to add a **branded, year-round, retail-grade** product line on top of seasonal field crops. Because Grow modules are sealed and HACCP-ready, the co-op can serve supermarkets and food-service buyers that won't touch open-field producers.

## What we typically deploy

- 4–8 **Grow** modules feeding a central packing room in a Shell.
- 1× **Flow** running a closed-loop water system at 5% of typical hydroponic draw.
- 1× **Cycle** turning crop scrap and packaging into biogas for the kitchen and biochar for the fields.
- **Core** runs lot-tracking and the supermarket EDI integration.

## Why co-ops choose AXAL

- The capex is **financeable per module**, not as a single $5M plant.
- Members can deploy a single Grow at their own farm for trial production.
- The data layer rolls up to the co-op's analytics, so quality is comparable across members.
