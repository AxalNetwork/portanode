## The problem with traditional field labs

Permitting, trenching, and shipping a custom-built field lab to a remote site is typically a 24–36 month process. By the time the trailers arrive, the grant cycle is often over. Trailers don't insulate, don't power their own analytics, and don't talk to a uplink without a separate IT contract.

## What an AXAL Research Lab gives you

- A **5-module stack** that ships factory-tested and stands up in under a week.
- **Lab-grade water** out of Flow — no shipping pallets of bottled water for glassware.
- **A BSL-2-ready Shell** with HEPA HVAC and optional negative-pressure conversion.
- **Two Volts** for genuine power redundancy through 90-day field campaigns.

## Where it ships well

Polar stations, high-altitude observatories, marine science, mineral-exploration camps, archaeology — anywhere the science is more interesting than the infrastructure.
