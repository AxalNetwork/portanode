## Pre-positioned response

We hold rotating Disaster Response inventories at three logistics hubs. A single phone call dispatches a stack within 72 hours, and any AXAL module fits a 747F bay.

Beyond emergency response, NGOs use AXAL for:

- **Primary-care clinics** under government health-ministry contracts.
- **School-in-a-box** deployments with the Learn module.
- **Refugee-settlement sanitation** with Flow + Cycle, eliminating septic trucking.
