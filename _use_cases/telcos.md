## Two patterns we see most

**1. Tower retrofit.** Replace ageing lead-acid + diesel at a remote tower with a single Volt. Typical OPEX reduction: 60–75% over five years. Same footprint, cleaner permits, fewer site visits.

**2. MEC at the edge.** Drop an Edge Compute stack next to a 5G site or a regional fibre PoP. 120 kW of IT load with N+1 cooling, dual uplink, and an SLA you can actually defend.

## What's in the stack

- **Edge Compute stack:** Core + 2× Volt + Shell (DC layout). Add another Volt for higher uptime targets, or a Flow for evaporative-cooling regions.
