## What guests notice

No generator hum at 3 AM. Hot showers without water-rationing. Fresh greens on the menu in midwinter. A reliable Wi-Fi signal in the lodge bar.

## What owners notice

- Capex is comparable to a traditional grid-tied build, but **OPEX drops 60–80%**.
- The brand story (zero-waste, energy-independent, locally sourced) writes itself.
- Permits are lighter — modules are removable, so many jurisdictions classify the build differently.

## Typical stack

- **Eco-Resort stack** for ~30 keys: Core + 3× Volt + Flow + Grow + Cycle.
- For larger properties (60–100 keys), parallel two stacks on a shared bus and add a second Cycle.
