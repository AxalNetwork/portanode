--- layout: page title: Financing permalink: /financing/ eyebrow: Commercial subtitle: Three ways to acquire AXAL — outright purchase, operating lease, and infrastructure-as-a-service. --- ## 1. Outright purchase Pay for hardware on milestone (30/30/40) and own the modules outright. Standard 36-month structural warranty, optional service contracts. - **Best for:** customers with capex budget, long-life deployments, government contracts that prefer ownership. - **Lead time:** standard. - **Tax:** depreciable in most jurisdictions. ## 2. Operating lease (24–60 months) We work with two specialty asset-finance partners (one in EU, one in North America) to offer **operating leases on AXAL hardware**. You pay a fixed monthly rate, and at end-of-term you can purchase, redeploy, or return. - **Best for:** customers who prefer OPEX accounting, IFRS-16 disclosures, fast project sign-off. - **Indicative pricing:** roughly 1.8–2.4% of hardware cost per month, depending on term and tier. - **Includes:** standard service contract; export controls apply. ## 3. Infrastructure-as-a-service For specific stacks (Edge Compute, Off-Grid Clinic), we offer **per-module monthly subscriptions** that include hardware, service, software updates, and a published SLA. - **Best for:** telcos, healthcare-system pilots, NGO program offices, R&D sandboxes. - **Indicative pricing:** $9k–$22k per module per month depending on configuration. - **Includes:** hardware, axalOS, NOC integration, on-call support, swap-out under SLA. ## What's the same across all three - **Same hardware.** Same FAT, same warranty terms. - **Same software.** axalOS releases the same regardless of how you paid for the metal. - **Same redeployment story.** All AXAL modules are built to be moved. ## Talk to us Procurement, treasury, and program-finance teams can email finance@axal.dev. We typically respond within one business day with a deal-structuring questionnaire.
