--- layout: page title: About AXAL permalink: /about/ eyebrow: Company subtitle: We make container-scale infrastructure modules so the people doing the world's hardest work can focus on the work, not the building. --- ## Why AXAL exists Field teams — researchers, doctors, miners, telco engineers, aid workers — spend an absurd share of their lives waiting for someone else to build the building they need. Permits, trenching, contractors who don't show, fuel deliveries that arrive a week late. We started AXAL with one bet: **if you make every piece of physical infrastructure ship on a flat-rack, you compress that timeline by an order of magnitude.** Eight modules later, we're still betting on it. ## Where we work - **Headquartered:** Rotterdam, Netherlands (engineering + EU operations). - **Manufacturing:** Eindhoven and Singapore. - **Logistics nodes:** Rotterdam, Singapore, Houston. - **Field ops:** ~40 staff embedded with customers across six continents. ## Who we are AXAL is a privately held company. The founding team has shipped infrastructure for a hyperscaler cloud, a humanitarian agency, and a Nordic telco. Our investors have committed for the long lifecycle of the asset — we are not the right kind of company to flip on an 18-month exit. ## How to talk to us - **General:** hello@axal.dev - **Procurement:** procurement@axal.dev - **Press:** press@axal.dev - **Field ops (24/7):** +31 10 555 0142 If you're a customer with a stack already deployed, your account engineer is your fastest path. axalOS includes a one-tap support button.
