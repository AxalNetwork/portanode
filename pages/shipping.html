Operations

Shipping anywhere modules can reach.

Every AXAL module is built to standard ISO 1496 dimensions. That means it ships on the same vessels, trains, and trucks that carry the rest of global trade — no special handling, no expensive permits.

Tier 1 · 42 ports

6 weeks · scheduled liner

Major ports across North America, EU, Gulf, East Asia, and Oceania. Inland delivery within 7 days of port arrival.

Tier 2 · 31 ports

8–10 weeks · feeder + barge

Secondary ports across Africa, South America, South Asia, and Pacific islands. Includes documentation for in-country customs clearance.

Tier 3 · case-by-case

12+ weeks · multimodal

High-altitude, polar, conflict-adjacent, and small-island sites. Air-freight surcharge available for Disaster-Response stacks.
